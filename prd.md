Phase 1: Clean PRD (Technical Specification)
The name of the app is called Venlidate.

1. App Goal
A SaaS tool that analyzes startup ideas against 7 proven business model fundamentals using AI, providing entrepreneurs with scored validation reports (0-100) to help them prioritize which ideas to pursue, avoid wasting time on non-viable concepts, and identify critical risks before quitting their jobs.

2. User Roles
Single role: Authenticated User

Can create and submit startup ideas for validation
Can view all their validated ideas in a dashboard
Can track iteration history as they refine ideas
Can see community benchmark percentiles
Can access pricing information (no payment required in MVP)

No admin role in MVP - All users have equal access during beta


3. Target Users (Tiered)
Primary: The Trapped Corporate Entrepreneur
Profile:

Age: 28-45
Current role: Mid-senior corporate (consulting, banking, tech, law)
Income: $100K-300K+
Psychographic: Golden handcuffs, dreams of "building something," researches side hustles at night, has 5-10 "ideas" in Notes app, paralyzed by analysis
Pain: Afraid to quit job for wrong idea; has limited nights/weekends to experiment; doesn't know which idea to pursue first
Example: You 3 years ago, McKinsey consultant researching edtech ideas

Secondary: The Serial Wantrepreneur
Profile:

Age: 25-40
Has tried 2-3 failed side projects
Follows startup Twitter, consumes entrepreneurship content
Pain: Keeps picking "cool" ideas that don't make money; can't distinguish vitamins from painkillers
Example: Developer who built 5 beautiful apps with zero revenue

Tertiary: Early-Stage Founders (Validation)
Profile:

Already building but seeking external validation
Pre-revenue or early revenue (<$10K MRR)
Pain: Questioning if they should pivot or persevere
Use case: Quarterly "health check" on business model viability
